= Urd

_Urd is one of the three Norns in Norse mythology — the one who weaves the past._

A civilisation-scale planetary simulation engine. Models physical and civilisational processes over a 10 000-year
horizon — tectonics, climate, hydrology, geology, weather, vegetation, fauna, and the civilisations that emerge from and
are constrained by them.

Godot 4 is the rendering layer. The simulation runs headless.
